Introduction

Most of my childhood I was raised on a rescue ranch surrounded by horses and animals in general. Growing up around horses I understood them and how much they impacted myself and my family emotionally. When we lost my father I couldn't afford neither horse therapy or human therapy so my own horses were my own emotional therapy. Back then horse therapy wasn't really known or studied either, and if it was it focused more on people with phyiscal disabilties. Nowdays I work at a equine therapy and rescue, where I get to really study and undertand horse therapy in all aspects whether its emotional or phyiscal.

Previous research of equine psychotherapy or horse therapy has been focused on people with disabilities, PTSD and or eating disorders, however over the years horse therapy has grown for those with depression, anxiety, and anger management. This research study advances in previous research to answer the question; what are the advantages of equine psychotherapy for adolescents who struggled with, depression, anxiety, and trauma in the Portland Metro area?
